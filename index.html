Honda Fit

The Good: It's capacious, fuel economy is outstanding and it's pretty fun to drive.

The Bad: The engine could sound better, and the infotainment system could use an upgrade.

The Bottom Line: If your new car needs to be cheap and effective, good luck topping the Fit

Honda Jazz

The Good: Big audio and air-con controls. Acres of space for bodies. Magic Seats equates to van-like load-lugging ability.

The Bad: Not quite as cute as before. Interior is very black and grey. iPod connectivity available overseas, but not here.

The Bottom Line: The Jazz impresses with its abundance of space, cheery outlook on life and clever engineering.

Honda Civic

The Good: The Civic Type R has the cargo space and on-road comfort to function very well as a daily, but it's still boatloads of fun.

The Bad: It looks like teenagers designed it, and the infotainment system has aged like a wine with a bad cork.

The Bottom Line: If you can handle the looks, the Civic Type R will always leave you smiling, no matter the destination.
